Objective:
Aim for Bingo first by completing any line!
Right now there are 63 possible tasks to complete!

Rules

  • Preferably 2-4 players max at one time or try solo and see how fast you can get Bingo! (Only singleplayer works at the moment)
  • You have a FREE space in the middle
  • Everyone starts in Florawish Stylist Guild
  • No teleporting - you have to walk/bike everywhere , you can teleport to the Sea of stars from anywhere - however you can only teleport back to the Styling Guild
  • The bicycle outfit is allowed - Motorbike can be used only if everyone has the outfit to make it fair
  • You can use any in game resource to help you locate things - The collections tab will be your best friend
  • You are allowed to search things up if necessary. Ex. Where is (NPC) located
  • Banned Ability Outfits:
    • Snowy Ballad (Mermaid - water walking)
    • Adventure Ride (motorcycle) Unless EVERYONE agrees and has the outfit
    • Rainfall and Blossoms (Witch floating - since there is an extra dash)
    • Forever Band (Cat purification outfit - Extra Dash)
    • Enchanted Encounter (Large grooming area)
  • All you need to do is get 1 line in any direction to win! - Challenge Mode: Try for any 2 Lines, or the letter X
